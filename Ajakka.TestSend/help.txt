Commands:
help
Shows the list of supported commands.

clear
clears screen

send 
Sends a notification about a new device. MAC, IP, and Hostname are generated randomly.

send <MAC>
Sends a notification about a new device with the provided MAC. IP, and Hostname are generated randomly.

send <MAC> <IP>
Sends a notification about a new device with the provided MAC and IP. Hostname is generated randomly.

send <MAC> <IP> <Name>
Sends a notification about a new device with the provided MAC, IP, and Hostname.


Press Ctrl+C to exit
